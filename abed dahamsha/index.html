FC Bayern Munich

FC Bayern Munich is a professional sports club based in Munich, Bavaria. It is best known for its professional men's football team, which plays in the Bundesliga, the top tier of the German football league system. Bayern is the most successful club in German football history, having won a record 33 national titles, including 11 consecutively since 2013, and 20 national cups, along with numerous European honors. In 2020, Bayern Munich, under the head coach Hans-Dieter Flick, won the sextuple and became the first team to win the UEFA Champions League by winning all matches.
